31A few such exercises have already been performed: Carroll (2001) shows that the time-series dynamics of micro-level disagreement in inﬂation expectations matches reasonably well the predictions of his baseline SI/sticky expectations model. Coibion and Gorodnichenko (2012) compare the implications of sticky expectations to those of a Kalman ﬁlter, and conclude that the dynamics of the cross section indicate substantial rigidity in beliefs whichever framework is used.
