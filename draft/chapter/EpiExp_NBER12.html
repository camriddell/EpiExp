9Of course as is the case in many settings, the cause of changes in beliefs, here the election outcome, is also a potential cause of changes in objective economic circumstances. The paper makes use of its rich data to provide strong evidence that diﬀerences in circumstances such as local economic conditions or hedging needs are not driving the results.
